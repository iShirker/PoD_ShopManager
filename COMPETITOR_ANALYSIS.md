| Competitor | Type | Primary Focus | Pricing (approx.) | Key Strength |
|------------|------|----------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Printify** | PoD + marketplace | Fulfillment, catalog | Free – $29/mo | Catalog size, multi-provider |
| **Printful** | PoD + fulfillment | Fulfillment, branding | Free – custom | Quality, own fulfillment |
| **Gelato** | PoD + global print | Global fulfillment | Free – $129/mo | Global reach, B2B |
| **AnywherePOD** | PoD management | Listings, personalization | $9.99 – $39.99/mo | Etsy/Shopify, personalization |
| **Persify** | Automation | Etsy order automation | Free + paid | AI personalization automation |
| **Customily** | Personalization | Mockups, customization | $49/mo + per-item | Real-time preview, POD links |
| **Dynamic Mockups** | Mockups | Mockup generation | Free – $19/mo | Mockup API, bulk |
| **Craftybase** | Inventory | Inventory, Etsy sync | $15 – $210/mo | Inventory, multi-channel |
| **EverBee / Etsy calc** | Tools | Etsy fees, profit | Freemium / one-off | Fee & profit calculators |

**PoD Shop Manager differentiators (summary)**  
- **Unified platform**: SKU + listings + orders + profitability + discounts + (future) mockups in one app.  
- **Solo seller focus**: Pricing and UX tailored to 1-person operations.  
- **Supplier-agnostic**: Works with Gelato, Printify, Printful; we don’t fulfill.  
- **Transparent pricing**: Subscription $19.99–$99.99; per-transaction only for heavy resource use.

## 5. Competitor Strengths (Summary)

- **Printify**: Huge catalog, many providers, simple free tier.  
- **Printful**: Own fulfillment, quality, branding.  
- **Gelato**: Global network, B2B, higher-tier plans.  
- **AnywherePOD**: Etsy/Shopify listings, personalization, bulk, grouped listings.  
- **Persify**: Etsy-focused AI automation, personalization → print-ready.  
- **Customily**: Real-time customization, mockups, POD integration.  
- **Dynamic Mockups**: Mockup API, bulk generation, templates.  
- **Craftybase**: Inventory, Etsy/Shopify sync, multi-channel.  
- **EverBee / calcs**: Etsy fees and profit only; no POD or listing management.

## 6. PoD Shop Manager Positioning

| Dimension | PoD Shop Manager | Typical Competitor |
|-----------|------------------|--------------------|
| **Audience** | New → established **solo** PoD sellers | Mix of solo and teams |
| **Revenue range** | $0 – $10k+/month | Often higher or undefined |
| **Pricing** | $19.99 – $99.99/mo | $9.99 – $210+/mo, or custom |
| **Scope** | Listings + SKU + orders + profitability + discounts + (mockups) | Usually 1–2 of these |
| **POD** | Aggregator (Gelato, Printify, Printful) | Often single POD or own fulfillment |
| **Fees** | Subscription first; transaction only for heavy use | Often sub + transaction or usage-based |
| **Mockups** | Own implementation (API-friendly for future) | Often third-party or separate product |
